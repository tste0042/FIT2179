Where Australia Runs

Participation, access, and outdoor running conditions across Australia.

Running is one of Australia’s most accessible physical activities, but participation, organised running access, and outdoor conditions vary across the country.

01

How popular is running?

AusPlay estimates that 3.74 million Australian adults participated in running or jogging in 2025. The first section places running against other common activities, then shows how participation differs by age and gender.
